Overview

The vehicle appears as a modern iteration of the original Vigero, having an enlarged profile and aggressive styling. The front of the car is largely dominated by a lower grille on the center and smaller intakes on the outer edges, followed by a black extender/splitter with angular shapes around the front quarters. The upper area is composed of a narrow upper grille on the center that bears the manufacturer logo, while the outer sides are occupied by a set of modern headlights, consisting of dual circular units and narrow strips. The hood lid bears distinctive formations around the center and has a cut-out section for the black engine intake, while the section below the windshield is finished in black and mounts the corresponding wipers. The same hood opens to reveal a large engine with chrome cam covers and a riveted plate on the frontal section, together with numerous badges on the same engine.
